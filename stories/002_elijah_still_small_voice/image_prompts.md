# Story 002: Elijah and the Still Small Voice
## Image Prompts — 30 Scenes (script-synced)

### Style Foundation (apply to every prompt)
biblical epic oil painting, Rembrandt chiaroscuro lighting, cinematic composition,
ancient Levant 9th century BCE, photorealistic painting, masterwork quality,
deep shadows, warm amber and gold tones, sacred and austere atmosphere

### Negative Prompt (apply to all)

low quality, blurry, extra limbs, deformed hands, medieval European architecture

### Elijah Character Reference (use across all Elijah scenes)
lean weathered bearded man in his forties, dark intense eyes, rough dark robes,
angular jaw, deeply lined face, the bearing of a man absolutely certain of his authority
